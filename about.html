The King Sports Story

King Sports Kashmir was born in 2018 in the heart of Srinagar, Jammu & Kashmir. What started as a passion for cricket and a deep respect for Kashmir's rich willow tradition has grown into one of the most trusted cricket bat brands in the region.

Our founder, growing up in Srinagar, watched the valley's legendary willow craftsmen pour their skill into every bat. That heritage inspired King Sports Kashmir — a brand that honors the artistry of Kashmir while delivering bats that perform at the highest level.

We believe every cricketer, from the gully to the ground, deserves a bat that feels alive in their hands. That's why we source only the finest Kashmir Willow, shape it with traditional techniques, and finish each bat with meticulous care.

The King Sports Timeline

2018
The Beginning

King Sports Kashmir is founded in Srinagar with a simple mission: bring premium handcrafted cricket bats to every cricketer in J&K.

2019 – 2020
Building the Craft

We refined our willow selection process, partnering with trusted suppliers in the Kashmir Valley to source the finest Grade A willow.

2021
Going Online

Launched our WhatsApp ordering system, making it easier for cricketers across J&K to order directly from their phones.

2022 – 2023
Regional Recognition

Crossed 300+ bats sold, earning the trust of club cricketers, schools, and academies across Srinagar, Baramulla, and Anantnag.

2024
The King's Era

500+ bats crafted, new product lines launched, and a full eCommerce store to serve cricketers everywhere. The story continues.

Our Process

Kashmir Willow Craftsmanship

Every King Sports bat goes through a careful process to ensure it performs at the highest level.

🌲

Willow Selection

We hand-select each willow cleft for grain quality, density, and performance potential. Only the best make it into a King Sports bat.

🪵

Shaping & Pressing

Our craftsmen shape each bat by hand, ensuring the perfect profile, edges, and spine for maximum power and durability.

🔩

Handle & Balance

Premium cane handles wrapped in high-quality grips provide the right balance and feel for every playing style.

✨

Finishing

Each bat is sanded smooth, oiled, and finished with our King Sports stickers. Ready to perform from the first ball.
